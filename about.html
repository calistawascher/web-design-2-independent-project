Our Heritage

Steam Beans is a family owned and run coffee shop based in Ottawa. Steam Beans first opened its doors back in 2009, offering fresh, roasted coffee beans, teas and baked goods to locals.

About Our Coffee Beans

We are passionate about coffee. That means providing you with the most delicious, quality coffee is our utmost priority. All of our coffee is sustainably grown, organic and is fairtrade cerified. Our coffee comes from several parts of the world including Columbia, Costa Rica, and Guatemala. All of our coffees are available for purchase and delivery through our shop.

About Our Tea Leaves

As much as we love coffee, tea will always be a soft spot in our heart. All of our loose leaf teas are organic and fairly traded. Our earl grey teas are from Sri Lanka. Our green teas are from Japan. Our black teas are from Indonesia. All of our teas are available for purchase and delivery through our shop.
